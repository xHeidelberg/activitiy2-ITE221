Philippine College Scholarships

A comprehensive guide for incoming freshmen in Public and Private Institutions.

Government (Public) Programs

These are funded by the Philippine government and are usually applicable to State Universities and Colleges (SUCs) or specific priority courses.

DOST-SEI

Science & Tech

For students gifted in science and mathematics who wish to pursue careers in S&T fields.

  • Monthly Stipend (₱7,000+)
  • Tuition Subsidy
  • Book Allowance
Apply via DOST →

CHED Tulong Dunong

General

Financial assistance for deserving students enrolled in CHED-recognized programs.

  • Available for SUCs and LUCs
  • GPA requirement: 85% above
  • Income-based eligibility
Apply via CHED →

Private & Corporate Foundations

Private scholarships often cover full tuition in top-tier private universities (like ADMU, DLSU, UST) and provide a path to employment.

Foundation Coverage Target Schools
SM Foundation Full Tuition + Allowance Partner Private/Public Universities
Aboitiz Foundation Full Tuition + Board Exam Fees Engineering & Business Degrees
Security Bank Full Tuition + Stipend Selected NCR/Regional Partners

Standard Application Requirements

Most PH scholarships will require the following documents. Prepare these early!

○ Certified True Copy of Grades (Form 138)
○ BIR Income Tax Return (ITR) of Parents
○ PSA Birth Certificate
○ Certificate of Good Moral Character
○ Barangay Residency Certificate
