02. Cognitive Science Study Systems

Stop "passive reading." High-achievers use active systems to retain 90% of what they learn.

The Feynman Technique

If you can’t explain it simply, you don’t understand it. Try teaching a concept to a classmate using no jargon.

Spaced Repetition (SR)

Don't cram. Review notes 24 hours later, then 1 week later, then 1 month later to move info to long-term memory.

Blurting Method

Read a page, close the book, and write down everything you remember. Fill in the gaps with a different colored pen.
