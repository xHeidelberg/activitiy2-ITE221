Why Focus on the Philippines?

Education in the Philippines is a powerful tool for social mobility, yet many students miss out on Public grants (like CHED) or Private scholarships (like SM or Aboitiz) simply because they didn't know the deadlines or requirements.

Did you know? Thousands of scholarship slots in the Philippines go unfilled every year because of a lack of centralized information.
